My Favorite ADVENTURE Manhwa

The S-Class That I Raised

The S-Class That I Raised is about a F-rank Hunter Han Yoojin who, due to his rank, brought his S-rank brother, Han Yoohyun, down. In the world of The S-clas That I Raised, hunting was the easiest and the best way of making money. With the cost of risking your life.

Although Yoojin is an F-class, he did exactly that at the cost of his brother's life who sacrificed himself.

After avenging his brother, Yoojin receieved an item that can grant any wish besides the resurrection of the dead. He then wish to go back in time and from there, that was when his adventure started.


As for my favorite character, it is obviously the main character, Han Yoojin . Peace, yoojin's first pet, being a close second.
